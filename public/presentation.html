The Problem

Running a gym still means fighting your own paperwork.

Attendance

Manual registers and missed check-ins — no real record of who actually showed up.

Payments

Cash collection and follow-ups for dues that quietly slip past due date.

Records

Member details spread across notebooks, spreadsheets, and WhatsApp chats.

Branches

No single view across locations — every branch runs on its own system.

The Solution

One dashboard. Every branch. Every member.

  • A single platform to run your entire gym or club — front desk to back office.
  • Web-based, so it works on any device your team already has.
  • Purpose-built for gyms, sports clubs & fitness studios in Pakistan.
01 · Members

Every member profile, always up to date.

  • Complete profiles — photo, contact & emergency info in one place.
  • Digital membership cards, shared straight to WhatsApp.
  • Status tracked automatically — Active, Expired, Suspended.
02 · Attendance

Check-in that takes a second, not a signature.

  • Members scan a QR code at the front desk.
  • Automatic check-in and check-out — no manual logging.
  • Works with any simple USB scanner — no expensive hardware.
03 · Billing

Invoices that generate themselves.

  • Auto-generated invoices for memberships & renewals.
  • Accept Kuickpay, cash, bank transfer & more.
  • Full payment history and pending dues, per member.
04 · Branches

One login. Every location.

  • Manage unlimited branches from a single account.
  • Branch-wise members, staff, attendance & revenue.
  • Built for gym chains and franchises.
05 · Team

The right access, for the right role.

  • Role-based permissions — Owner, Admin, Manager, Staff, Trainer.
  • Trainer-member assignments & trainer attendance.
  • Commission and salary tracking, built in.
06 · Scheduling

Bookings that can't double-book.

  • Group fitness classes with capacity limits.
  • Court & facility booking for sports clubs.
  • Real-time availability — no scheduling conflicts.
07 · Progress

Progress your members can actually see.

  • Health profiles — goals, medical notes, measurements.
  • Weekly progress check-ins, tracked by trainers.
  • Visual reports members stay motivated by.
08 · Insights

Know your gym's numbers, in real time.

  • Real-time revenue & attendance trends.
  • Expiring-membership alerts before you lose a member.
  • Exportable payment reports.
09 · Membership Plans

Every plan, priced your way.

  • Create unlimited plans — Monthly, Quarterly, Annual, PT-included & more.
  • Set your own pricing and duration for each plan.
  • Active members and revenue tracked automatically, per plan.
